FAQ

Questions you might want answered

What is the Wriggle project platform?

Wriggle is a free and open-source project management platform designed to help teams and individuals stay organized, collaborate efficiently, and manage tasks with ease.

Who is this platform for?

It’s for anyone who needs to manage projects—whether you’re a solo freelancer, a startup team, or a large organization.

What are the costs?

The platform is completely free to use and will always be free! Since it is open-source, there are no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium features behind a paywall.

Will there ever be a paid version?

No. The platform is open-source, meaning it is built and maintained by the community. There are no plans to introduce a paid version.

How can I contribute to the platform?

You can find the source code on our Gitlab repository. Feel free to fork the project, make changes, and submit pull requests!
